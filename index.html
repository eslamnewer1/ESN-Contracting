About ESN

Established in 2012, ESN For Integrated Contracting is a professional engineering firm specialized in delivering end-to-end construction solutions. Originating from the expertise of Eng. Eslam Sobhy Nower, we have managed 60+ projects across residential, commercial, and governmental sectors.
