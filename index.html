About ESN

Established in 2012, ESN For Integrated Contracting is an integrated contracting and engineering management firm specialized in delivering comprehensive construction solutions across all project phases — from planning and technical coordination to execution and final handover.


Originating from the professional practice of Eng. Eslam Sobhy Nower Integrated Contracting Office, the firm has developed through more than a decade of practical construction experience, successfully managing and delivering over 60 projects across residential, commercial, administrative, educational, governmental, and public service sectors.
